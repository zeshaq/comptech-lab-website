AI workloads run best on the platform your security and operations teams already trust. We
stand up **OpenShift AI on the OpenShift fleets we deploy**, then layer inference, retrieval,
and agentic patterns on top.

## What we deliver

- **OpenShift AI deployment.** Operator install, GPU operator and node feature discovery,
  workload tenancy, model registry, dashboards. Integrated with your existing GitOps, secrets,
  and observability stack.
- **Inference platforms.** vLLM, Triton Inference Server, TGI, NVIDIA NIM, Ollama — chosen and
  sized to your workload mix. Quantization, batching, GPU sharing, KV-cache tuning.
- **RAG architectures.** Vector store selection, embedding pipelines, chunking strategy,
  retrieval evaluation. Connected to your enterprise content sources behind identity.
- **Agentic AI.** MCP servers, tool wiring, multi-agent patterns, evaluation harnesses,
  guardrails. The hard part is rarely the model — it's the boundary contracts and rollback.
- **NVIDIA AI Enterprise.** Licensing posture, NIM catalog onboarding, AIWorkbench, BlueField
  DPUs where relevant.

## What we do not do

- Train foundation models from scratch.
- Build the application UX or product layer on top of the AI platform — that's your team.
- Procure GPUs.

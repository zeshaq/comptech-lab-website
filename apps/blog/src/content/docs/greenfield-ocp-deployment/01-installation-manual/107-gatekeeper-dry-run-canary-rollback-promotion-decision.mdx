## Promotion Decision

Do not promote `K8sDisallowPrivilegedContainers` to deny yet.

The reason is operational, not a policy failure:

- rollback and restore are proven;
- dry-run status reports zero violations;
- the canary namespace has no meaningful workload population yet;
- the hub remains intentionally no-policy;
- Gatekeeper still lacks durable disconnected image mirror coverage.

The current state is a good safe pause point: one scoped dry-run policy is
live, rollback is proven, and no deny enforcement is active.

## Residual Risks

Gatekeeper deny enforcement is still not active.

The dry-run canary has not produced real workload signal.

Gatekeeper image supply still depends on current pull/cache readiness rather
than durable mirror coverage.

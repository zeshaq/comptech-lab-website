## Schedule Policy

Created one resource-only Velero Schedule per cluster:

```text
platform-resource-daily
```

| Cluster | Cron | StorageLocation | TTL | Snapshots | FS backup |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `hub-dc-v7` | `15 2 * * *` | `hub-dc-v7` | `168h0m0s` | disabled | disabled |
| `spoke-dc-v7` | `45 2 * * *` | `spoke-dc-v7` | `168h0m0s` | disabled | disabled |

The schedules include all namespaces as Kubernetes resources only. Volume
snapshots and filesystem backup are disabled for this first recurring gate.

## Operational Notes

```text

restores.velero.io
deletebackuprequests.velero.io
```

Short names are ambiguous in this environment.

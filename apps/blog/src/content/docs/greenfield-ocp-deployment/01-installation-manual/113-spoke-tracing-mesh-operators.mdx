## Preflight

Preflight checked hub and spoke health, OADP, package and catalog resolution,
related image prefixes, existing Subscriptions, CSVs, namespaces, CRDs, and
excluded operands. GitOps render and per-resource server-side dry-run were
completed before the push.

One first preflight pass saw a transient spoke ODF status reporter pod in
`ContainerCreating`; a recheck found no non-running pods.

## Promotion Decision

Keep the operator-only install. It establishes tracing and mesh operator
readiness without activating trace storage, collectors, a mesh control plane,
Kiali, Jaeger, mesh membership, telemetry pipelines, tenant namespaces, or
sample applications.

Rollback is GitOps-first. Full CRD, webhook, console-plugin, or cluster-scoped
cleanup requires a separate issue.

Follow-up planning: Batch 4 app-platform operators were deferred by user
decision in [114 App-platform batch deferral](/docs/greenfield-ocp-deployment/installation-manual/app-platform-batch-deferral/).
